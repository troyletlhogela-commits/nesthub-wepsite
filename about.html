We Are NestHub 🏠

"Find a place. Make it home."

Our Story

We are three university students from Botswana who experienced firsthand how difficult it is to find safe, affordable accommodation. Every year thousands of students arrive in Gaborone and face the same painful struggle — searching through Facebook groups, dealing with unreliable landlords, and wasting weeks trying to find a place to call home.

We asked ourselves one question — why doesn't Botswana have a proper platform for this? We couldn't find a good answer. So we decided to build one ourselves.

NestHub was born out of that frustration — and out of a genuine desire to make life easier for every student and young professional in Botswana looking for a place to call home.

🎯

Our Mission

To connect property owners, students and individuals through a simple digital platform that makes finding housing fast, safe and accessible for every Motswana.

🌍

Our Vision

To become Botswana's central digital hub for housing — a platform where anyone can find a home, buy furniture and book moving services all in one place.
